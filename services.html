Crane Maintenance & Servicing

We offer Crane Repair and Maintenance Services for the host of industries. Our effective maintenance services allow our users the easy and hassle free usage of Cranes. Supported by highly skilled engineers, our services are acclaimed for accuracy and speediness. When it comes to maintenance services, our technician and service engineers are not afraid of getting their hands dirty. Repair and maintenance services include - Breakdown maintenance, Periodic Preventive Maintenance, Annual Maintenance Contracts or AMC and Predictive maintenance.

We also undertake replacement of worn out parts, improvement in existing design to reduce need for frequent change of parts, Replacement of Brake Disk and Brake liners, refurbishment of motors and gearboxes, replacement of wire rope and rope sheaves, , rectification of rail level difference.

Crane Modernisation

With years of experience, we are also your partner when it comes to crane modernisation. A retrofit, for example, requires a particularly large amount of specialist knowledge. It is not only important to systematically replace damage components but also to conduct modifications to retain compliance with the frequent changes in safety regulations, from limit switches to automatic monitoring systems. We also undertake up gradation of complete electrical system of crane. This may include up gradation of Electrical Drives, PLC and refurbishment of complete electrical system.Thanks to our know-how, we can ensure that your current crane systems continue to work productively and without any problems for many more decades

Study of Crane Operation to determine duty cycle, Crane audit, Visual observation, dimensional verification, NDT testing of critical members to determine residual thickness of load bearing structures, measurement of speeds of Hoist, measurement of motor currents, measurement of crane girder deflection under load and analysis of structures using computerised software such as STAAD & ANSYS are some of the technique that we employ to determine whether the crane is suitable for upgradation.

Inspection Reporting

We specialize in Health Check Up/ Safety Audit of Ship to shore, RTG, RMG, MHC, PHC, ELL cranes and spreaders. Our team has solid experience to carry out Health Check Up of cranes. Periodic Inspection of Cranes is to be carried as per OEM standard.Depending on duration of operation and the factory operating conditions, the cranes need to be inspected by an experienced technician or an Expert Engineer.

Expert Engineers are engineers experienced in the design, construction or maintenance of cranes with sufficient knowledge of the regulations and standards. The expert Engineers should be in a position to judge the safe condition of the crane and to decide which measures shall be taken in order to ensure further safe operation.

Periodic Health Check-up of Cranes highlights the shortcomings in the existing system and the need for repair/ replacement of parts. It also helps in planning for spare parts as well as the repairs can be done during planned shutdown rather than attending to breakdowns.
