ABOUT PORTECH

PORTECH is a multi-discipline engineering services company. PORTECH is committed to enhance customer satisfaction by delivering quality and timely services through a solution approach which requires intense involvement with clients to understand their needs and create solutions to meet those needs. PORTECH offers Crane Maintenance & Servicing, Crane Modernisation, erection and commissioning, Inspection & Reporting, and Dismantling & Disposal.
